How to buy and sell cryptocurrencies via Kraken exchange?

Before you can start to buy and sell cryptocurrencies, you need to deposit the funds in the Kraken account. Here are the elaborated steps to buy and sell the cryptocurrencies:

Instantly buy crypto on Kraken

The quickest way to buy, sell and convert the crypto quickly all you need to do is to click on the “Buy crypto” button on the top of the page. You can see the applicable fees after selecting the “Preview buy” option before you enter the transactions.

Trading with Kraken exchange

Step 1: Select the currency pair

  1. First of all, you need to know which assets you wish to Kraken exchange.

  2. Once you know which pair you will be trading, you can select them from the quote bar.

  3. Remember the first currency in a pair is the base currency and the second currency is the quote currency.

  4. In the Buy order, you have to pay the quote currency and receive the base amount.

  5. Similarly, you sell the base currency in the sell order option.

  6. From here you can easily select any of the trading pairs.

Step 2: Filling out the forms

  1. Once you have selected your trading pair, visit the “New Order” page and select the “Simple” form.

  2. On the page, you will see three text boxes and the option to use a market or limit order.

  3. Follow the on-screen instructions if you wish to complete the forming process.

Step 3: Submit order:

  1. Once you have decided to look for a market or limit order, you can easily select the choice.

  2. Once things look good to you just click on the large buy/sell button and you will be directed to the order confirmation page.

  3. On the screen, you need to click on the “Skip order confirmation” box to be checked.

Step 4: The extra volume is not visible and included in order

  1. Just wait for order execution, if the market order is selected, the trade will happen quickly and the balance will change.

  2. The funds can be easily withdrawn to trade again.

  3. You can view a successfully closed market order in the “Orders” section.

  4. Just limit the orders, you can view the status in the “Orders” tab.

  5. If your order is not executed yet then you can “Cancel the order” by clicking on the “x” button under the “New & Open orders” option.
